Louis Coleman

Hi, I'm Louis Coleman, born and raised in Tampa, Fl and lived there until October of 2019 when I moved to Seattle, WA.

Growing up I always had a huge facination in computers, mainly gaming. This continued as I grew older and at times had an impact on school. I attended Idaho State University for Civil Engineering, where I left after a few months into my second year. When I returned home I began to research different programming languages. At the time I was working at RinkSide Sports, a pro shop in the Brandon Ice Sports Forum, where I had mainly played growing up, and the recently opened AdventHealth Center Ice. I then transitioned to my familys construction company Pelican Building Contracting where I worked my way up over the years from being a Laborer, Forklift Operator, and eventually the Office Manager and Bookeeper. I was also playing Rugby for the Bay Area Pelicans based out of St.Pete, I was also the President for a couple of years. This took up a lot of my time and though I took some classes in community college I was never fully able to push myself to take the classes and fulfill my desire to pick up coding. When I moved to Seattle, with the help of my Girlfriend, Shannon, I was able to find the coding bootcamp and decided that this was my opportunity to go for working in an industry which I had a huge passion in! I owe a huge thanks to my family, Shannon, and her family for helping me along the way to get in the position where I am and can't wait for what comes next!
